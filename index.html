What We Offer

Charter Services Tailored to Every Journey

From solo business travel to full group charters, each flight is planned and executed with meticulous care by our dedicated team.

01

Business Charter

Productive, private, and punctual. Board on your schedule with direct routing, dedicated crew, and onboard conference capability for executives who cannot afford delays.

02

Leisure & Lifestyle

Arrive refreshed at a safari lodge, coastal retreat, or international city. We curate every detail of the in-flight experience to set the tone for your journey.

03

Group Charter

Corporate retreats, family milestones, or sporting delegations — our larger-cabin aircraft carry groups of up to 19 passengers in comfort, on a unified itinerary.

04

Air Cargo

Time-critical freight across Africa. From pharmaceutical samples to high-value parcels, our cargo charters offer tracked, secure, door-to-ramp solutions.

05

Medical Evacuation

Swift, fully equipped air ambulance flights coordinated with medical personnel. Available 24 hours, with immediate response protocols across East Africa.

06

Concierge Services

Ground transfers, hotel bookings, in-flight catering preferences, and destination arrangements — handled as one seamless experience by your dedicated flight coordinator.
