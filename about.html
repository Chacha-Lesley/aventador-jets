Our Story

Built on Africa's
Skies

Royal Luxury Jet was founded in Nairobi with a singular conviction: that discerning African travellers deserve a private aviation standard equal to — and informed by — the continent they fly above. Since our founding, we have connected clients across 40 countries, maintained a perfect safety record, and grown to become East Africa's most trusted charter operator.

Our base at Wilson Airport places us at the heart of Kenya's aviation community, close to the routes, airstrips, and landscapes that define travel in this part of the world.

What We Stand For

The Principles That Govern Every Flight

These are not aspirations. They are operational requirements that every member of our team — from engineers to cabin crew — is held to on each and every departure.

  1. 01

    Safety Is Non-Negotiable

    Every aircraft is maintained to KCAA and international ICAO standards. No flight departs unless every system has been signed off by a licensed engineer.

  2. 02

    Absolute Discretion

    Client identities, itineraries, and preferences are held in the strictest confidence. Our team signs NDAs as standard. Your movements are yours alone.

  3. 03

    Precision in Every Detail

    From catering instructions to runway slot timings, we plan with exactness. When the unexpected occurs — weather, ATC delays — our experience converts disruption into minimal inconvenience.

  4. 04

    Africa-First Expertise

    Our pilots and coordinators have deep, lived knowledge of African airspace — its bush strips, weather patterns, and the nuances of inter-continental routing that no algorithm can replicate.
